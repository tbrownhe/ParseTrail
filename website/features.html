Features

ParseTrail is a free, privacy-first personal finance app. Statement imports are painless, spending is auto-categorized locally, and your data stays on your own machine. Create a free account only to fetch new plugins from the ParseTrail server; no financial data is collected, just basic contact info for updates. Open source and community driven.

Know Your Net Worth

Bring in bank, card, loan, and investment statements in minutes. ParseTrail visualizes assets, debts, and net worth so you can see progress without spreadsheets.

Local-only by design: your balances, categories, and models stay on disk. The free account is only for plugin updates; your finances never leave your device.

Track Spending Without Effort

Smart categorization learns your habits and keeps improving. Spot the categories that matter—groceries, dining, travel, subscriptions—and adjust in seconds.

Free forever and open source: no subscriptions or upsells. Inspect, fork, or improve the code.

Stay In Control

Recurring-charge detection and anomaly spotting help you catch surprises early. Export reports for budgeting, taxes, or to share with a partner.

Built for usability: clean views, fast navigation, and reports you can act on. Need a new parser? Submit a statement securely for plugin development, or build your own and open a PR on GitHub.
